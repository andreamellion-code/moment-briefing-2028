Persona 01 · Strategic answer · AIRFREE marketplace

Mei Lin unlocks 27% of AFKL marketplace value, blocked today by 3 missing essentials.

Today's blockers

  1. Local PSPs missingNo Alipay, no WeChat Pay — checkout fails for Mainland China HNW shoppers.
  2. No Mandarin marketplaceCatalog only in EN / FR — product descriptions unreadable for top FFP CN customers.
  3. Limited delivery networkNo hotel delivery, no arrival airport pickup — last-minute purchases blocked.

Recommended actions

  1. Alipay + WeChat Pay integrationNative CN PSPs from seatback · no card entry.Q3 2026
  2. Mandarin catalog & CN HNW verticalCurated European luxury · Chinese HNW storefront.Q4 2026
  3. Hotel & cross-continent deliveryLast-mile partnership · arrival pickup network.H1 2027

Persona 02 · Strategic answer · AIRFREE marketplace

Beatriz cluster (group leisure, non-EU → EU) unlocks 6% marketplace value via group purchases and themed souvenirs.

Today's blockers

  1. No Group CartFour friends ordering separately lose group discounts and shared checkout.
  2. No "Europe Souvenirs" verticalLooking for typical EU products — only generic duty-free catalog available.
  3. Delivery to Asia / Americas unmanagedVague delays, hidden customs fees, no last-mile transparency.

Recommended actions

  1. Group Cart / Travel TogetherShared basket · group discount · split checkout.Q3 2026
  2. Europe Souvenirs verticalThemed storefront · producer stories · regional curation.Q4 2026
  3. International delivery partnershipTransparent customs · tracked last-mile to Asia / Americas.H1 2027
