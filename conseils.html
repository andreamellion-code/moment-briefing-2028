Exhibit 02 · Market structure

The Air France-KLM market is atomised across 6 typologies, each 15–19% — no single segment dominates.

Operational baseline

  1. Busy ambitious · 19%Flying = task in busy schedule · India, Brazil, Korea
  2. Functional minded · 19%Flying = least exciting · Japan, NL, FR, DE

Experience seekers

  1. Travel lovers · 15%Journey = destination · Brazil, Italy, Korea
  2. Premium seekers · 15%Flying = routine · China dominant

Defensive / opportunistic

  1. Cautious insecure · 16%Air travel = exceptional · France, Germany
  2. Online price hunters · 17%Means of transport · Nordics, NL, USA, UK
Source · Air France-KLM customer study, Dec 2015 · n ≈ 5,000 passengers · 13 markets Briefing 01 · 03 / 15
