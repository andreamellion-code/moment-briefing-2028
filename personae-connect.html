  3. No unified Mobile Companion PWACheck-in + WiFi + flight info + lounge fragmented across 5 apps.

  1. Business Premium WiFi tierQoS + VPN unblocked + auto-eligible top FFP via Account & Loyalty SSO.Q3 2026
  2. Mobile Companion PWA1-click connect + flight info + lounge wayfinding + boarding pass.Q4 2026
  3. Lounge Digital CompanionWiFi continuity airport ↔ lounge ↔ aircraft.H1 2027
